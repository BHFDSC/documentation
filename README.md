# Heath Data Science Team Documentation

This site provides documentation for the work of the Health Data Science (HDS) Team at the British Heart Foundation Data Science Centre, Health Data Research UK. It brings together guidance, technical notes, methods descriptions, and other supporting material for our projects: https://bhfdsc.github.io/documentation/.

## How to cite

Please cite uses of this documentation, e.g.:
“Methods and workflows were informed by the Health Data Science Team Documentation (British Heart Foundation Data Science Centre at Health Data Research UK), available at https://bhfdsc.github.io/documentation/, accessed 29 July 2025.”
For additional information see our Publication and Dissemination Policy (https://bhfdatasciencecentre.org/publication-and-dissemination-policy/)
